Lens Care Guide

How to Care for Your Lenses

Proper lens maintenance ensures clear vision and extends the life of your eyewear

01

Clean with Microfiber Cloth

Use a soft microfiber cloth to gently wipe lenses. Avoid paper towels or tissues.

02

Use Lens Cleaner Solution

Apply a small amount of lens cleaner and gently wipe with a microfiber cloth.

03

Store in Protective Case

Always store your glasses in a hard case when not in use to prevent damage.

04

Avoid Extreme Temperatures

Keep lenses away from heat sources to prevent coating damage and warping.
