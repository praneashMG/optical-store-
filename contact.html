FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book online through our website, call us directly, or visit our center in person.

What services do you offer?

Comprehensive eye exams, prescription eyewear, contact lenses, LASIK, cataract surgery, and pediatric care.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Contact us for specific coverage details.

How often should I get an eye exam?

We recommend an annual eye exam for most adults, or as advised by your optometrist.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations for certain services. Contact us to schedule a video appointment.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Same-day cancellations may incur a fee.
